About Me

Hello! I'm Ajito Nelson, an IT Specialist (Big Data) with a passion for Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning. As a native Tetum speaker, I bring a unique perspective to Timor-Leste's growing tech ecosystem.

Currently, I work at Viettel Timor (TELEMOR), specializing in designing scalable data pipelines, optimizing the Viettel Data Platform, and transforming complex datasets into actionable insights.

My expertise includes:

  • Dashboard Development & Automation – Enhancing decision-making with data-driven insights
